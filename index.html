CRC and more… 關於 CRC，以及更多

CRC 探索在戰爭、災難與科技風險日益交疊的當代情境中，何謂數位韌性。並以鬆散連結的 co-lab 形式運作，持續促成研究與實作之間的協作。連結研究、實作與藝術，CRC 探討後人類戰爭如何在軍事、公民與數位領域中被想像與實踐。

CRC explores digital resilience under contemporary conditions in which war, disaster, and technological risk increasingly overlap. CRC runs as a loosely connected co-lab that sustains collaboration between research and practice — allowing diverse forms of expertise, from social sciences and information engineering to artistic practice, to intersect.

OpenFun 歐噴有限公司
