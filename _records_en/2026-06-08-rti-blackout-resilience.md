> “But when a power-grid outage shuts down cellular base stations, how can a household power its Wi-Fi router? Michael explained that routers generally require only 10 to 28 volts, so vehicle battery modules, such as electric-vehicle batteries, are a particularly convenient and readily available source of electricity in an emergency and can ensure that internal communications networks continue to function soundly.”

BUT, but, but—the situation above is only an emergency simulation. Do not reach for the nearest electric-vehicle battery station just yet; be careful not to break the law 🔋
