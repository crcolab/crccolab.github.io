Thank you to everyone who took part in the “Offline Action”!

More than **180 turns**, **67 minutes** of relay pedaling, and a total distance of **9.2 km** on a flywheel generator sent a steady stream of resilience messages to the Meshbridge message board. Dozens of participants at the venue also connected successfully to the message board over Wi-Fi. Better still, Reticulum robots exchanged **6,759 messages** over two days—enough to make us wonder whether they were secretly developing a social circle of their own!?

CRC hereby formally declares: this Offline Action was mission accomplished!
