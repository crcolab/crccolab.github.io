**Time**｜Saturday, June 27, 14:00–16:00
**Venue**｜Co-Space

Taiwan is an island beset by disasters: everyone fears for themselves, yet everyone also trains themselves. Approaching the subject through civil defense, digital resilience, and primary healthcare, this talk discusses how a new kind of organizer outside traditional organizational structures can develop agency in high-risk environments, and how a sense of crisis can be transformed into training, knowledge, and mutual-aid networks.
